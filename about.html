--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About

I am a Machine Learning Research Scientist at Scale AI, where I work on deep learning, natural language processing, optical character recognition, and form understanding. I'm also affiliated with Masakhane, an open source and distributed research effort for NLP for African languages and ML Collective, an independent nonprofit ML research organization. I'm also collaborating with Doug Downey at AI2 on scientific article summary generation.

Previously, I worked on fake speech detection and synthesis at AI Foundation. I completed my MS in Computer Science at the Courant Institute at NYU in the CILVR group focusing on deep learning applied to natural language processing and advised by Kyunghyun Cho and Sam Bowman. Before this I completed my B.A. in Statistics and M.S. in Computer Science focusing on machine learning and natural language processing at Northwestern University. My advisor was Doug Downey.

Broadly, my research interests are:

  • Machine Learning
  • Causality
  • Transfer Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Sequence Generation
  • Representation Learning
